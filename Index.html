Best places to visit in Bangladesh

Cox’s Bazar

Cox’s Bazar is a town on the southeast coast of Bangladesh. It’s known for the longest Sea Beach in the world. Aggameda Khyang monastery is home to bronze statues and centuries-old Buddhist manuscripts. South of town, the tropical rainforest of Himchari National Park has waterfalls and many birds. North, sea turtles breed on nearby Sonadia Island.

Sundarbans

The Sundarbans is a cluster of low-lying islands in the Bay of Bengal, spread across Bangladesh, famous for its unique mangrove forests. This active delta region is among the largest in the world, measuring about 40,000 sq km. The famous Royal Bengal Tiger's origin is this Sundarbans.

Sylhet

Sylhet is famous for beautiful tea gardens, rain forests and beautiful green sceneries. Lawachara National Park is a major national park and nature reserve in Bangladesh. The park is located at Kamalganj Upazila, Maulvi Bazar District in the Sylhet region of the country. It is located within the 2,740 ha West Bhanugach Reserved Forest.
